The Project

Isoshealth helps to achieve your goals with THE POWER OF 3 - Dietician, Physiotherapist, Psychologist. It aims at building a web platform which provides interactive online sessions with a team of leading healthcare professionals.

My Role

Business insights & ideation

Partnered with our business stakeholders to uncover insights and translate concepts into features that address their mission and vision.

Design Execution

Translated the ideas and user journeys into paper mockups, then digital prototypes. Developed interactive prototypes for feedback.

Living Style Guide

Created a living style guide that helped the design and development team to be on the same page

User Testing

Partnered with the other UX designer to carry out user testing session to refine our product.

Front-end Tech Stack

Partnered with the developers to lay down the front-end tech stack using react, SASS, reactstrap, webpack and node.js

Coding templates

Coded the view into reactstrap using HTML and SCSS and handed over the templates to the development team.

Our Solution

We aimed to build an easy-to-use web interface that would allow clients and health care practitioners to have effective video consultations. The clients would be able to choose a dedicated team and carry out online consultations according to their preferred time.

The solution aimed to help the practitioners to collaborate effectively in a multidisciplinary team and help clients to acheive their health goals.

The Process

The expectation was to get the product out in the market within 3-4 months, so we decided to go for lean approach that includes rapid sketching, design mockups, prototyping and user feedback.

Our agile process for the product

1. Discovery Phase

We began the product discovery by understanding the user. A survey was carried out to understand questions related to the user's lifestyle, physical and mental health, emotional wellbeing, health goals, motivations, etc. A number of brainstorming sessions were carried out to understand the core aspects of the application, including, what are the business expectations, what the users want to achieve, types of users and how they would use the application, etc.

We also carried out some market research to understand the existing products. This helped us to understand what pain points are they trying to resolve and what they are not able to address.

Next, our sessions involved whiteboarding and sketching of key features which the clients wanted to implement.

2. Crafting the experience

After gathering insights from the previous phase, we assessed how best the features that the business aim to implement would add to the user experience of the product and help achieve their goals and their usersâ€™ goals. We came up with various users personas and then crafted user journeys for each of our personas to conceptualise the proposed functionality.

Overview of client user journey to join the platform

3. Sketching

We explored various ideas for layout, navigation and interaction by sketching and creating paper mockups.

We went through these ideas and weighed them against our requirements, design patterns, basic design principles and our constraints to decide on the best.

4. Design and interaction

We went through the brand guidelines and designed the visuals of few key screens using appropriate colours, typography, and icons in Sketch.

After presenting those screens to the clients, we got quick feedback on the visual style and then designed the rest of the screens. We also got some valuable feedback by doing user testing on interactive prototypes.

Next, I created interactive prototypes in invision. Digital Prototypes helped me to get feedback and approval from clients and the senior leadership.

Designing the product: Sketch, Creating interactive prototypes: Invision

5. Coding templates

After incorporating feedback from the clients and seniors, I created the HTML and SASS templates in reactstrap which were handed over to the development team.

6. The Refinement

We invited some practitioners and carried out user testing on our development environment. We got some good insights which helped us to further refine the overall experience of the application.
