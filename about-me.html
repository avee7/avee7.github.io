👋🏼 I'm a product designer based in London with 5+ years of experience in designing delightful user experiences.

🌈 I believe in improving the lives of others through design. I care about consistency, scalability and reusability. These values are reflected in my design and approach.

🙌🏼 I believe that constructive feedbacks help us to grow and the most amazing designs are achieved by collaboration and multiple iterations. This always motivates me to do more and learn new things.

🌟 I care about the details and consider prototyping as an important skill which gives life to thoughts and helps to take decisions.

💻 My background in development helps me to have a natural inclination of designing reusable components.

🌿 Beyond work, I like long walks in nature, painting, gardening and yoga.

What do I do?

  • Create concepts and wireframes
  • Build interactive prototypes
  • Work closely with UX Researchers to design tests and transform research findings to design ideas.
  • Design systems
  • Create macro and micro interactions that make a difference
  • Work closely with technical team to implement designs
  • UI design
  • Coding in HTML, CSS and Javascript
  • Visual Design

What fascinates me these days?

I’m learning to explore the power of the data analytics to learn how can we better address the needs of our users. I feel it’s an exciting area which helps to build metrics and measure success.
